Anoad 9: An teaghlach / Mo theaghlach

Talking about family.

Useful Sentences

How Many People Are There in Your Family?

Q: Cé mhéad duine atá i do theaghlach?

A: Tá triúr i mo theaghlach.

How Many Cousins Do You Have?

Q: An bhfuil colceathar agat?

A: Tá cúigear agam.

My Mother is … / My Mother’s Name is …

Is é Cian m’athair.

Ciara is ainm do mo dheirfiúr.

one to ten people: duine amháin/beirt/triúr/ceathrar/cúigear/seisear/seachtar/ochtar/naonúr/deichniúr

Vocabulary

fada: á / é / í / ó / ú
