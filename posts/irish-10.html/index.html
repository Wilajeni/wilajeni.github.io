Anoad 10: An Corp

Talking about body parts.

Useful Sentences

What’s Wrong with You?

Q: Cad tá ort? (What’s on you?)

A: Tá pian i mo cheann. (Pain’s on my head.)

A: Ghortaigh mé mo cheann. (My head hurts.)

A: Tá tinneas cinn orm. (Headache is on me.)

I am…

Tá brón orm. (I am sorry.)

Tá áthas orm. (I am happy.)

Tá ocras orm. (I am hungry.)

Tá tart orm. (I am thirsty.)

Tá an fliú orm. (I have a flu.)

Tá slaghdán orm. (I have a cold.)

Tá fiabhras orm. (I have a fever.)
