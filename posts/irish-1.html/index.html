Anoad 1: Dia duit!

Say hello in Irish.

Useful Sentences

Hi!

Dia d(h)uit! (Hello to one person)

Dia daoibh! (Hello to a group of people)

Dia is muire duit! (Reply to someone’s greeting)

Bye!

Slán! (Bye!)

Slán go fóil! (See you until next time!)

What’s your name?

Q: Cad is ainm duit? (What’s your name?)

A: Mise …, seo … (I am …, this is …)

Deas bualadh leat! (Nice to meet you!)

What’s your (phone) number?

Q: Cén uimhir atá agat? (What’s your number?)

A: M’uimfir ná … （My number is …)

Courtesy phrases

Go raibh maith agat. (Thank you.)

Gabh mo leithscéal. (Excuse me.)

Le do thoil. (Please.)

Más é do thoil é. (Please.)
